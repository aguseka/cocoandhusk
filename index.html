About Us

Coco&Husk.com is a website that owns and managed by PT. Satria Tirta Nusa, from Indonesia. The company was founded back in 2015. As a distributor of the biggest bread producer in Indonesia: "Sari Roti" for Jakarta Area, serving around 5 million customers.

Ever since having an opportunity to manage a community farm and a briquette manufacturer in Eastern Java, later the operation moved to Bali and start selling bananas, mangos, charcoal, and briquette for some chain restaurants at the local market.

However, as the local market shrinks due to pandemics, the management decides to expand and start international trading.
